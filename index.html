Frequently Asked Questions

What is crop resistance?

Crop resistance refers to the ability of a plant to withstand pests and diseases.

How do hybrid crops compare to traditional crops?

Hybrid crops often have better resistance to pests, while traditional crops may adapt better to local conditions.

What are the benefits of integrated pest management?

Integrated pest management combines various strategies for sustainable crop protection.
